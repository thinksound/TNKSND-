What this tool deliberately surfaces

  • ① ATF / mic mismatch sensitivity — errors fill nulls; worse at higher order.
  • ② WNG collapses with order — trade WNG against directivity.
  • ③ MVDR is the reference — differential rarely beats it in practice.
  • ④ Noise, reverb & reflections fill nulls — on-paper rejection isn't real-world.
  • ⑤ 3D reality — 2-mic/planar beams can't resolve elevation.

Honesty note: multiplicative beams are nonlinear detection patterns, not clean linear audio estimators. Metrics are pedagogical proxies.
